Oh Hell! is a trick-taking card game for 2–7 players. You can play solo against AI opponents or pass the device between friends in pass-and-play mode.

How to Play

Each round, players are dealt a hand of cards and must bid how many tricks they expect to win. Score points by hitting your bid exactly — bid too high or too low and you miss out. The number of cards dealt changes each round, keeping every hand unpredictable.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I remove ads?

Open the Settings menu (gear icon) and tap Remove Ads. This is a one-time in-app purchase that permanently removes all interstitial ads.

How do I restore a purchase?

On the Settings screen, tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in to the same store account used for the original purchase.

What is the Hook Rule?

When enabled, the last bidder (the dealer) cannot bid a number that would make the total bids equal the number of tricks available. This ensures someone is always set each round.

What is Blind Bidding?

In Blind Bidding mode your cards are hidden while you bid, adding an extra layer of challenge.
