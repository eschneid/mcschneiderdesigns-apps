Hearts — Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 17, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 17, 2026

Hearts is a card game built by McSchneider Design Studios. This policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

No account required. Hearts does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to play.

Anonymous gameplay analytics. We collect anonymous, non-identifiable data about how the app is used, including:

  • Game configuration (number of players, variant settings)
  • Game and round completion events
  • Whether ads were shown or removed

This data is not linked to your identity and cannot be used to identify you.

Advertising data. The app displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain device information and identifiers to serve relevant ads. Please see Google's privacy policy for details on how AdMob handles data.

Purchase information. If you purchase the “Remove Ads” option, the transaction is processed entirely by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment information. We only receive confirmation that the purchase was completed.

What we do not collect. We do not collect your name, email, location, contacts, camera or microphone data, or any other personally identifiable information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To understand how the app is used and improve gameplay
  • To serve advertisements to users who have not purchased ad removal
  • To honor your “Remove Ads” purchase across app sessions

We do not sell or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

The app relies on the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy.

  • Google Firebase Analytics (anonymous usage analytics) — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google AdMob (advertising) — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Play Billing (in-app purchases) — Google Payments Privacy Notice
  • Expo / EAS (build and distribution platform) — expo.dev/privacy

4. Advertising and Opt-Out

The app displays interstitial ads between games via Google AdMob. You can remove all ads permanently by purchasing the “Remove Ads” option within the app’s Settings menu. You may also opt out of personalized advertising at any time through your device settings under Google › Ads › Opt out of Ads Personalization.

5. Data Retention

Anonymous analytics data is retained by Google Firebase according to their standard retention policies. Your “Remove Ads” purchase status is stored locally on your device and is not stored on our servers.

6. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, please contact us and we will address it promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
