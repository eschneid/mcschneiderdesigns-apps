Hearts is the classic trick-avoidance card game for 4 players. Play solo against AI opponents or pass the device between friends in pass-and-play mode.

How to Play

Players are dealt 13 cards and pass 3 cards to an opponent each round. The goal is to avoid taking penalty cards — each heart is worth 1 point and the Queen of Spades is worth 13. The player with the lowest score when someone reaches 100 points wins.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Shooting the Moon?

If one player takes all 13 hearts and the Queen of Spades in a single round, they “shoot the moon.” Instead of scoring 26 points, all other players receive 26 points. It’s a high-risk, high-reward move.

How does passing work?

At the start of most rounds, each player selects 3 cards to pass to an opponent. The pass direction rotates each round: left, right, across, then no passing (a hold round). Passing is your chance to shed dangerous cards like the Queen of Spades or high hearts.

Why can’t I lead hearts?

Hearts cannot be led until they have been “broken” — meaning a heart was discarded on a previous trick. On the very first trick of a round, hearts and the Queen of Spades cannot be played at all.

How do I remove ads?

Open the Settings menu (☰ icon) and tap Remove Ads. This is a one-time in-app purchase that permanently removes all interstitial ads.

How do I restore a purchase?

On the Settings screen, tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in to the same store account used for the original purchase.
