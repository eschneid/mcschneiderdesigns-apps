Caverna Scorekeeper is a score-tracking companion app for the board game Caverna: The Cave Farmers. Track scores for up to 6 players across the Base Game and The Forgotten Folk expansion.

How to Use

On the Setup screen, choose your expansion variant and enter player names, then tap Enter the Cave. During the game, expand each player's card and use the +/− buttons to record scores in each category. When the game is over, tap Save & End Game to store the results.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which expansions are supported?

The app supports two variants:

  • Base Game — standard scoring across all cave and resource categories
  • The Forgotten Folk — adds Mushrooms (+1 VP each) and Gem Fruits (+2 VP each)

The scoring legend on the Setup screen updates automatically when you switch between variants so you can review all categories before starting.

What categories are scored?

The app covers all standard Caverna scoring categories:

  • Dwellings — points based on room tiles built in your cave
  • Furnishing Tiles — victory points from furnished rooms
  • Gold, Rubies, Donkeys, Dogs — 1 VP each
  • Sheep, Pigs, Cattle — 1 VP each
  • Grain, Vegetables, Flax, Fruit — 1 VP each
  • Dwarves — 1 VP each
  • Written Scrolls — 1 VP each
  • Begging Markers — −3 VP each

How do I enter Furnishing Tile points?

Furnishing Tile scoring varies by room — use the +/− buttons to enter the total VP from all furnished rooms combined rather than counting room types individually.

Where is my game history?

All saved games appear on the History tab. Tap any game to see the full score breakdown per category. To delete your entire history, tap the Clear History button at the top of the History screen.

What does the Stats screen show?

The Stats tab shows per-player statistics including wins, win rate, average score, average placement, best and worst scores, and consistency. It also shows all-time records: highest score ever, closest finish, biggest blowout, most consistent player, and top average scorer.

My scores look wrong after saving. What happened?

Double-check that you selected the correct expansion on the Setup screen before starting — The Forgotten Folk adds extra scoring categories. The active expansion is shown at the top of the Scoring screen for reference.
