Spades is the classic 4-player partnership trick-taking card game. Play as North/South against East/West, or pass the device between friends in pass-and-play mode.

How to Play

Players are dealt 13 cards and bid how many tricks they expect to win. Partners’ bids are combined into a team bid. Spades are always trump. Meet your team’s bid to score points — fall short and you lose them. First team to the target score wins.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Nil bid?

A Nil bid means you’re committing to take zero tricks for the round. Success earns your team a bonus; taking even one trick results in a penalty. Blind Nil is the same bet made before seeing your cards — with a higher reward and higher risk.

What are bags?

Bags are tricks taken beyond your team’s bid. Each bag is worth 1 point, but accumulating 10 bags triggers a 100-point penalty. Consistently overbidding will cost you.

What is the Board Minimum rule?

When enabled, a team must bid at least 4 tricks total per round. The second bidder’s minimum is automatically adjusted so the team cannot go below 4.

How do I remove ads?

Open the Settings menu (☰ icon) and tap Remove Ads. This is a one-time in-app purchase that permanently removes all interstitial ads.

How do I restore a purchase?

On the Settings screen, tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in to the same store account used for the original purchase.
